07 / FAQ

Questions.

What does the Free tier cover?

5 outreach actions per day with a 50 lifetime cap, 500 prospect registrations, and 1 project. No card required. Once you've sent 50 outreach actions total or want more daily volume, upgrade to a paid plan from your dashboard.

Do I need Claude Code?

Yes. LeadAce is a Claude Code plugin — it runs inside Claude Code on your Mac. You'll need a Pro or Max plan from Anthropic. Both work.

Why macOS only?

LeadAce depends on tools and workflows built for macOS. Windows and Linux support is not currently planned.

How is this different from Apollo or Instantly?

Those tools blast the same template to hundreds of prospects. LeadAce has Claude read every prospect's website before writing, producing one genuine email per prospect. Lower volume, much higher signal.

Will this flag me as spam?

LeadAce sends from your own inbox at a reasonable cadence, writing genuinely personalized messages. Deliverability depends on your domain reputation — we don't share infrastructure with other users.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard — no lock-in, no cancellation fees. Annual plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled at renewal.

What if you shut down the hosted service?

The full backend is on GitHub. You can deploy your own copy to Cloudflare + Supabase at any time and keep running with the same plugin. Your data is portable — see self-host.md.

Who built this?

SurpassOne Inc., a one-person software company based in Tokyo. LeadAce is the tool we built to run our own outbound — now shipped as a product.
